Tours in this country

Riga

City Information: Riga, the capital of Latvia, is known for its Art Nouveau architecture, medieval Old Town, and vibrant cultural scene.

Tour Description:
Experience the cultural and architectural highlights of Riga.

Old Town: Stroll through the cobblestone streets and visit landmarks like St. Peter’s Church and the House of the Blackheads.
Art Nouveau District: Admire the stunning architecture along Alberta Street.
Riga Central Market: Explore one of Europe’s largest markets housed in old Zeppelin hangars.
Latvian National Opera: Attend a performance or tour the historic building.

Expect: Architectural marvels, cultural experiences, and historical exploration.

Liepaja

City Information: Liepaja is known as the "City where the wind is born," situated on the western coast of Latvia by the Baltic Sea. It has a rich history, vibrant cultural scene, and beautiful beaches.

Tour Description:
Explore Liepaja’s musical heritage and maritime charm.

Karosta Prison: A guided tour of this former military prison offers a unique glimpse into the region's history.
Seaside Park: Relax and enjoy the scenic beauty of the park and its stunning beach.
St. Nicholas Orthodox Naval Cathedral: Marvel at this architectural masterpiece.
Concert Hall Great Amber: Discover the modern cultural hub of Liepaja.

Expect: Historical insights, seaside relaxation, and cultural experiences.

Daugavpils

City Information: Daugavpils is Latvia’s second-largest city, renowned for its rich cultural diversity and historic architecture.

Tour Description:
Experience the multicultural heart of Latvia.

Daugavpils Fortress: Visit the 19th-century fortress, a significant historical monument.
Mark Rothko Art Centre: Explore the works of the famous artist and other exhibitions.
Church Hill: See various religious buildings that reflect the city’s diverse culture.
Dubrovina Park: Relax in the green oasis in the city center.

Expect: Art and culture, historical landmarks, and serene parks.

Ventspils

City Information: Ventspils is a port city known for its family-friendly attractions, well-maintained parks, and maritime history.

Tour Description:
Delve into Ventspils’ blend of modernity and history.

Ventspils Beach: Enjoy a day at one of Latvia’s Blue Flag beaches.
Ventspils Castle: Tour the medieval castle and its museum.
Open-Air Museum: Explore the maritime heritage and traditional Latvian lifestyle.
Children’s Town: Perfect for families, offering various activities and playgrounds.

Expect: Family fun, historical exploration, and beach activities.

Cesis

City Information: Cesis is a historic town known for its medieval castle and beautiful natural surroundings.

Tour Description:
Discover the medieval charm of Cesis.

Cesis Castle: Tour the medieval castle and its museum.
Gauja National Park: Explore the park’s natural beauty and hiking trails.
St. John’s Church: Visit one of the oldest churches in Latvia.
Cesis History and Art Museum: Learn about the local history and art.

Expect: Medieval history, nature exploration, and cultural insights.
