Narva

City Information: Narva is known for its strategic location on the border with Russia and its impressive castle.

Tour Description:
Discover the historic and strategic significance of Narva.

Narva Castle: Tour the medieval castle and its museum.
Narva River Promenade: Walk along the scenic riverside.
Narva Alexander’s Cathedral: Visit the historic cathedral.
Kreenholm Textile Factory: Learn about the industrial history of Narva.

Expect: Historic exploration, scenic walks, and industrial heritage.

Tallinn

City Information: Tallinn, the capital of Estonia, is known for its well-preserved medieval Old Town and modern tech scene.

Tour Description:
Experience the medieval and modern charm of Tallinn.

Old Town: Walk through the UNESCO World Heritage site with landmarks like Alexander Nevsky Cathedral and Toompea Castle.
Kumu Art Museum: Explore Estonia’s largest art museum.
Kadriorg Palace: Visit the baroque palace and its gardens.
Tallinn TV Tower: Enjoy panoramic views of the city.

Expect: Medieval history, modern culture, and scenic views.

Parnu

City Information: Parnu is known as Estonia’s summer capital, famous for its beaches and spas.

Tour Description:
Relax and rejuvenate in Parnu.

Parnu Beach: Spend time on the sandy beach.
Parnu Mud Baths: Experience the therapeutic mud baths and spa treatments.
Parnu Old Town: Stroll through the charming old town with its wooden architecture.
Parnu Museum: Learn about the local history and culture.

Expect: Beach relaxation, wellness experiences, and cultural exploration.

Tartu

City Information: Tartu is Estonia’s second-largest city, known for its prestigious university and vibrant intellectual scene.

Tour Description:
Explore the academic and cultural highlights of Tartu.

University of Tartu: Visit the historic university and its botanical garden.
Town Hall Square: Explore the central square with its iconic Kissing Students fountain.
Estonian National Museum: Discover the history and culture of Estonia.
AHHAA Science Centre: Enjoy interactive exhibits and science displays.

Expect: Academic atmosphere, cultural landmarks, and interactive experiences.

Haapsalu

City Information: Haapsalu is a charming resort town in western Estonia, known for its picturesque old town, soothing mud baths, and vibrant cultural scene. It's a perfect destination for those looking to relax and soak in the tranquil atmosphere.

Tour Description:
Experience the serene beauty and rich history of Haapsalu.

Haapsalu Castle and Cathedral: Explore the medieval castle ruins and the adjoining cathedral, which hosts various cultural events and exhibitions.
Promenade: Stroll along the beautiful seaside promenade, taking in the scenic views and fresh sea air.
Kuursaal: Visit one of Estonia’s oldest wooden buildings, now a charming restaurant and concert venue.
Haapsalu Railway Station: See the historic railway station, known for its long covered platform and unique architecture.
Ilon’s Wonderland: Discover the world of Ilon Wikland, the illustrator of Astrid Lindgren’s books, in this delightful family-friendly attraction.
Mud Baths and Spas: Relax and rejuvenate in Haapsalu’s famous mud baths and wellness spas, which have been attracting visitors for centuries.

Expect: Medieval history, coastal beauty, cultural experiences, and relaxation in spas.
